## 4. The "Gold Standard" Prompt
**Persona:** *Who is Copilot? (e.g., "You are an expert Sales Assistant.")*
**Audience:** *Who is this for? (e.g., "For the Client and the internal Account Manager.")*
**Context:** *What info is needed? (e.g., "Using the transcript of this meeting...")*
**Objective:** *What to do? (e.g., "Draft a follow-up email summarising key decisions and next steps.")*
**Output:** *Format? (e.g., "Professional tone, bullet points, clear deadlines.")*

**> Final Prompt:**
> *"You are an expert Sales Assistant. Using the transcript of this meeting, draft a follow-up email for the Client. Summarise the key decisions made and list the next steps with owners. Keep the tone professional and concise."*
