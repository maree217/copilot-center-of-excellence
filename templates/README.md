## üìù How to Use These Templates

### 1. Download and Customize
All templates are provided in markdown format for easy editing. Copy the content and adapt to your organization's:
- Branding and terminology
- Specific requirements
- Organizational structure
- Industry context

### 2. Integrate with Your Process
Templates align with the [4-Pillar Implementation Framework](../docs/leadership-course/01-implementation-framework.md):
- **Getting Ready:** Use Readiness Assessment, AI Council Charter
- **Onboarding:** Use Scenario Definition Cards
- **Delivering Impact:** Use ROI Calculator
- **Extending:** Use Decision Trees for architecture choices

### 3. Share and Collaborate
Share templates with:
- Executive sponsors (ROI Calculator, AI Council Charter)
- Business stakeholders (Scenario Definition Cards)
- Technical teams (Decision Trees)
- Project managers (Readiness Assessment)
